Instructions:
1. Enter a name and hit submit.
2. Join a room with other players or create a room and wait for the match to begin!
3. In game, you are set as either a killer, a cop, or an innocent.
    a. As a killer, your objective is to kill all the innocent players without them determining who you are.
    b. As a cop, your objective is to determine through investigation and cooperation who the killer is and arrest him.
    c. As an innocent, your objective is to communicate with the other still-living innocent players and the cop (whose identity is unknown) to arrest all the killers. You may vote to kill one player every turn.
4. You can move 3000 meters each turn. Movement is based on the streets you walk to get to places. Cops can also choose to arrest a target player.
5. Hit end turn. This will refresh your movement and change the time from day to night or vice versa. (At night, your visibility is greatly reduced).
6. The game ends when either all killers are arrested or dead or all innocents and all cops are dead.
